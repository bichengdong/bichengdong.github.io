### 课程简介 | Course Description

入侵生态学是生态学专业的核心课程，主要介绍生物入侵的生态学原理。课程内容包括生物入侵的概念、机制与影响，以及入侵物种的管理策略。

*Invasion Ecology is a core course for ecology students that examines the ecological principles governing biological invasions. The course covers the concepts, mechanisms, and impacts of biological invasions, as well as management strategies for invasive species.*

### 课程目标 | Course Objectives

- 理解入侵生态学的基本概念和理论 / Understand fundamental concepts and theories of invasion ecology
- 分析植物入侵的机制 / Analyze mechanisms of plant invasions
- 评估入侵物种的生态影响 / Evaluate ecological impacts of invasive species
- 制定生物入侵的管理策略 / Develop management strategies for biological invasions

### 课程内容 | Topics Covered

- 生物入侵简介 / Introduction to biological invasions
- 繁殖压力与定殖 / Propagule pressure and colonization
- 物种特性与入侵性 / Species traits and invasiveness
- 生态系统脆弱性与可入侵性 / Ecosystem vulnerability and invasibility
- 竞争与生态位理论 / Competition and niche theory
- 干扰与入侵 / Disturbance and invasion
- 入侵物种的影响 / Impacts of invasive species
- 检测与监测 / Detection and monitoring
- 管理与控制策略 / Management and control strategies
- 气候变化与未来入侵 / Climate change and future invasions

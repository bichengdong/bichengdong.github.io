### Course Description | 课程简介

*Invasion Ecology is a core course for ecology students that examines the ecological principles governing biological invasions. The course covers the concepts, mechanisms, and impacts of biological invasions, as well as management strategies for invasive species.*

入侵生态学是生态学专业的核心课程，主要介绍生物入侵的生态学原理。课程内容包括生物入侵的概念、机制与影响，以及入侵物种的管理策略。

### Course Objectives | 课程目标

- Understand fundamental concepts and theories of invasion ecology
- Analyze mechanisms of plant invasions
- Evaluate ecological impacts of invasive species
- Develop management strategies for biological invasions

### Topics Covered | 课程内容

- Introduction to biological invasions
- Propagule pressure and colonization
- Species traits and invasiveness
- Ecosystem vulnerability and invasibility
- Competition and niche theory in invasion biology
- Disturbance and invasion
- Impacts of invasive species
- Detection and monitoring
- Management and control strategies
- Climate change and future invasions

### Reference Materials | 参考资料

- Elton, C.S. (1958). *The Ecology of Invasions by Animals and Plants*
- Davis, M.A. (2009). *Invasion Biology*
- Recent journal articles from *Oecologia*, *Ecology*, *Biological Invasions*, etc.

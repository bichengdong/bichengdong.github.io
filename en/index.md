## Welcome | 欢迎

My name is **Bi-Cheng Dong** (董必成), an Associate Professor at the School of Ecology and Nature Conservation, Beijing Forestry University.

## Research Interests

- **Macroecology**: Analyzing invasion mechanisms of exotic ornamental plants in China using global and regional plant databases
- **Experimental Ecology**: Using field surveys and greenhouse experiments to systematically investigate (1) ecological significance of clonal traits in invasive wetland plants and (2) potential impacts of native insect herbivores on wetland plant invasions

## Education & Experience

- **2014 – 2020** Lecturer, Beijing Forestry University
- **2009 – 2014** Ph.D. in Ecology, Beijing Forestry University

## Research Interests

**Macroecology & Invasion Risk Assessment**
Using global and regional plant diversity databases combined with statistical modeling to elucidate the naturalization and invasion pathways of exotic ornamental plants in China, providing a theoretical basis for early warning and management of biological invasions.

**Clonal Plant Ecology & Invasion Mechanisms**
Using clonal plants as a model system, integrating field surveys with greenhouse experiments to investigate (1) how clonal integration enhances invasive plant adaptability in heterogeneous environments; (2) the regulatory role of native insect herbivores in invasive plant establishment and spread.

## Research Interests

**Macroecology & Invasion Risk Assessment** — Using global and regional plant diversity databases combined with statistical modeling to elucidate naturalization and invasion pathways of ornamental plants in China.

**Clonal Plant Ecology & Invasion Mechanisms** — Employing clonal plants as a model system, integrating field surveys with greenhouse experiments to investigate clonal integration and herbivore regulation in invasive plants.

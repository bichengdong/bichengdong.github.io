    <h1>Bi-Cheng Dong</h1>
    <p class="profile-subtitle">Associate Professor · Master's Supervisor<br>School of Ecology and Nature Conservation, Beijing Forestry University</p>
    <p>
      My research focuses on <strong>biological invasion ecology</strong>, with an emphasis on understanding
      the mechanisms underlying plant invasions at global and regional scales. Current projects include
      analyzing naturalization pathways of ornamental plants in China using global plant databases,
      and investigating the roles of clonal integration and native insect herbivores in regulating
      wetland plant invasions.
    </p>

### Research Interests

- **Macroecology**: Analyzing invasion mechanisms of exotic ornamental plants using global and regional plant databases
- **Clonal Plant Ecology**: Investigating how clonal integration affects environmental adaptability of invasive plants
- **Invasion Ecology**: Studying interactions between native insect herbivores and invasive plants
